Fix Yourself a Chili with Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 pound(0.45kg) lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon(approx 5.69g) chipotle chile powder, or more to taste
  • 1 can(approx 793g) no-salt crushed tomatoes in puree
  • 1 can(approx 411g) no-salt-added beef broth
  • 1 cup(approx 128g) Rice
  • 1 can(approx 311g) low-sodium corn, drained

Steps

  1. Place ground beef, onion, and garlic in a lightly greased oven.
  2. Cook over medium heat until ground beef is browned, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  3. Add chili powder, tomatoes and beef broth and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add Rice and corn and cook another 7 minutes.
  • Chef's tip: Add pickled olives and Cheddar cheese to add little different taste to your spicy dish.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 471 calories, 31.1g protein, 34g carbohydrates, 15.9g fat, 79mg cholesterol, 405.8mg sodium
